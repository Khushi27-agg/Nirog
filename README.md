# 🐘 Nirog – Gamified Preventive Healthcare App

## 🌿 Overview

**Nirog** is a gamified preventive healthcare platform designed to make health education simple, engaging, and accessible. The app helps users build healthier habits through daily wellness insights, interactive quizzes, and habit-based routines.

At the heart of the experience is **Arogya 🐘**, a friendly elephant mascot dressed as a doctor who guides users through health tips, quizzes, and daily routines.

The goal of Nirog is to promote **preventive healthcare** by encouraging people to understand their bodies and adopt healthy habits before serious medical problems arise.

---

## ✨ Features

* 📚 **Micro Health Modules**
  Short lessons explaining important health concepts.

* 🧠 **Gamified Quizzes**
  Learn health knowledge through fun interactive quizzes.

* 🐘 **Arogya the Health Guide**
  A friendly elephant mascot that guides users through the app.

* 📩 **Daily Wellness Mail**
  Daily health facts and tips to promote preventive care.

* 🔥 **Habit Streaks & Progress Tracking**
  Encourage consistency with streaks and progress levels.

* 🌦 **Seasonal Health Insights**
  Health tips inspired by traditional lifestyle principles such as **Dinacharya** and **Ritucharya** presented scientifically.
